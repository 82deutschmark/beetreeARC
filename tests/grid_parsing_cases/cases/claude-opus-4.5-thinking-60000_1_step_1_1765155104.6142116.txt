1. **Marker position**: In Example 1, the marker (4s) is at the bottom row. In Example 2, it's also at the bottom. In the test, the marker is at the top (row 0).

2. **Stripe projection**: The marker defines a vertical stripe that extends through the entire grid.

3. **Transformation rules**:
   - The stripe appears as 4s where it passes through shapes
   - At "special" rows, shapes convert to 3s and the stripe shows 3s
   - Special rows are: (a) the row above where a shape first enters the stripe, and (b) the widest row of shapes crossing the stripe
   - For shapes that don't intersect the stripe, their first row becomes 3s
